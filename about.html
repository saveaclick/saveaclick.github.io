Don't click on that, we already did.
Fighting news media clickbait for better journalism.

According to SubredditStats.com, /r/savedyouaclick is ranked 258 for most subscribers! However, we are also ranked very low on engagement metrics, such as "Post Per Subscriber," "Posts Per Day" and "Comments." We expect the low engagement is due to the difficulty posting on the subreddit, as we enforce strict url and formatting guidelines. We do not want to change our archive or formatting rules, but we want to provide a tool that makes it easier to post content on the subreddit.

This project aims to improve the user experience of /r/savedyouaclick by providing a progressive web app and extensions that simplify posting to the subreddit. Interested developers are welcome to message /u/kuhnie for discord access.
